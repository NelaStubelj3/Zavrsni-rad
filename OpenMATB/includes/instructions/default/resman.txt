<h1>Presentation of Tasks</h1>

<p>In the resource management task, participants must limit the leakage of the two main reservoirs (A and B) using a set of backup reservoirs and pumps, each with specific power levels.</p>

<p>The participant's task is to turn on/off the numbered pumps to bring the A and B reservoirs as close as possible to their normal levels.</p>

<p>Occasionally, pumps may malfunction.</p>
